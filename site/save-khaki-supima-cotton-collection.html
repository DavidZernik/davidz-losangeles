Save Khaki

Save Khaki website - collection page

This project was part of the Save Khaki e-commerce site, where I worked on a team maintaining and evolving the storefront. I personally built the Supima collection page to showcase a premium product line with a strong emphasis on photography, merchandising, and clear navigation. The site ran on Shopify, and my role was to translate design and merchandising requirements into a responsive, production-ready page that worked across the browsers and devices common at the time.

From a technical standpoint, the work focused on Shopify’s theming system, Liquid templates, and front end HTML and CSS. I implemented the collection layout, handled responsive behavior, and tested the page to ensure consistent rendering and performance. The result was a polished, maintainable collection page that fit cleanly into the existing e-commerce experience.
