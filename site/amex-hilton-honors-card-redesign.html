American Express Partnership Landing Page

This project was a mobile-first partnership landing page for American Express and Hilton Honors, designed to explain card benefits and guide users through a card design update without disrupting their experience. I built the page as a fully responsive layout, translating a dense set of product details, FAQs, and support content into a clear, scannable flow that worked equally well on desktop and mobile. The goal was to reduce confusion while keeping the messaging aligned with both brands.

On the technical side, I focused on responsive layout, performance, and cross-browser consistency using modern HTML, CSS, and grid-based layouts. I tested the page across common devices and browsers to make sure typography, spacing, and content stacking behaved predictably. The result was a flexible, maintainable landing page that could support ongoing content updates without breaking the user experience.
