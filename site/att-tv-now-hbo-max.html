AT&T

AT&TTV NOW/HBOMAX

This project was a static promotional email for the AT&T TV Now and HBO Max partnership, built to showcase a content grid of featured shows while keeping the message clear and visually punchy. I translated the creative into a responsive email layout that held its structure across desktop and mobile, and ensured accessibility through proper alt text, readable typography, and a logical content order.

On the technical side, the template was optimized for performance and deliverability by compressing and balancing image assets, keeping a healthy image to text ratio, and supporting mobile specific image swaps. It was tested across major email clients and devices, resulting in a reliable, lightweight campaign email that balanced strong branding with accessibility and inbox friendly performance.
