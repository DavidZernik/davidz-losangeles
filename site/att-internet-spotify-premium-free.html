AT&T and Spotify partnership

This project was a static promotional email announcing the AT&T and Spotify Premium partnership, designed to highlight the offer with a clean, typography driven layout and strong brand presence from both partners. I translated the creative into a responsive email that held up across desktop and mobile, while making sure the message stayed clear, readable, and accessible through proper alt text and structured content flow.

The build focused on efficiency and inbox reliability, with optimized image assets, careful image to text balance, and mobile specific image swaps where needed. The template was tested across major email clients and devices to ensure consistent rendering, resulting in a lightweight, deliverable friendly email that balanced performance, accessibility, and visual impact.
