DirecTV

DIRECTV and NBC partnership

This project was a static promotional email for the DIRECTV and NBC partnership, built to showcase network content with a strong, image forward layout while staying dependable across inboxes. I handled the build end to end, translating the creative into a responsive template that preserved the visual impact on desktop and mobile, while also meeting accessibility requirements through proper alt text and clear reading order.

The email was tuned for performance and deliverability by optimizing image assets, keeping a healthy image to text balance, and using mobile image swaps where needed. It was tested across major email clients and devices to ensure consistent rendering, resulting in a lightweight, standards compliant template that balanced branding, accessibility, and reliability.
