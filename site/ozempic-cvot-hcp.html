Ozempic Email

This project involved building a highly regulated pharmaceutical email for Ozempic focused on presenting CVOT study data and key safety information to a clinical audience. I handled the full front end email build, translating a dense, data-heavy design into responsive, email-safe HTML and CSS that could reliably render across major clients. The layout needed to balance marketing content with mandatory prescribing and safety sections, while keeping the hierarchy clear and the reading experience usable for busy healthcare professionals.

On the technical side, the work emphasized strict compliance and QA, including CAN-SPAM considerations, accessibility best practices like proper alt text, and defensive coding for inconsistent client behavior. I tested the template using Litmus and Email on Acid to validate rendering, spacing, and typography across devices and inboxes. The final result was a compliant, production-ready template that could support complex medical content without sacrificing reliability or readability.
