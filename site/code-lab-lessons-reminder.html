Email for coding school

This project was a fully custom marketing and progress reminder email for a coding school, designed and built around 2018 to encourage students to complete upcoming lessons. I handled both the visual design and the front end email development, creating all of the assets in Photoshop and translating the layouts into responsive, email-safe HTML and CSS. The content combined personalized messaging with course tiles and clear calls to action, which was a fairly modern pattern for education emails at the time.

From a technical perspective, the focus was on reliable rendering across the major email clients and devices that were common in that era. I tested and refined the build to account for client limitations, image handling, and layout fallbacks. The result was a cohesive, on-brand template that balanced personalization, usability, and the constraints of email in 2018.
