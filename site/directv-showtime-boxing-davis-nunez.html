DirecTV

DIRECTV and Showtime partnership email

This project was a static promotional email for the DIRECTV and Showtime partnership, focused on delivering a bold, image led announcement while staying reliable across inboxes. I handled the layout, responsive behavior, and accessibility details, including proper alt text and a clear content hierarchy, while keeping the design aligned with brand guidelines and readable on mobile and desktop.

On the technical side, the build was optimized for faster load times and deliverability by compressing images and keeping a healthy image to text balance. The template was tested across major email clients and devices, with mobile image swapping in place to ensure consistent rendering and performance.
